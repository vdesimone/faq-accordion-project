FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes! Frontend Mentor is free and offers free projects for all levels.

Does Frontend Mentor offer a variety of projects?

Yes! Frontend Mentor offers projects from level "Newbie" to "Guru"! It's the perfect website to grow and learn through projects that progress in difficulty.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

If you get stuck on a challenge, you can submit your code with your questions in the Discord, where the community can help give you viable feedback!
